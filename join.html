How can I get involved?

1. Become a member of CAL by emailing cal.actuarial.internalvp@gmail.com or by finding our Internal Vice President at the next event and paying $15 for a semester membership and a CAL pin!

2. Attend CAL's events by looking out for event details sent through the mailing list

3. Join our mailing list by signing up here or at one of our events

4. Join our Facebook and LinkedIn group by requesting to join the Cal Actuarial League group on Facebook and LinkedIn, where you will have the opportunity to connect with other CAL members and alumnus!
