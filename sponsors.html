Corporate Sponsorships

We gratefully acknowledge the support from our generous sponsors!

Thank you for your interest in establishing and/or strengthening your partnership with Cal Actuarial League, a professional student organization for aspiring actuaries at University of California, Berkeley. As the only actuarial student organization on campus, Cal Actuarial League is increasingly recognized as the ultimate resource for students interested in the actuarial profession at UC Berkeley. Year after year, our students remain highly desired by various firms across the nation for their strong command in data analysis, research, and communication.

As our corporate sponsor, we make it our top priority to connect you with UC Berkeley students. Your relationship with us could start with displaying your company logo and link on our website, and it could also be as large as being a major sponsor of our events, such as our annual Actuarial Case Competition. To request a copy of our Corporate Sponsorship Package or to direct any other inquiries, please feel free to contact our External Vice President at externalvp@actuary.berkeley.edu, or President at president@actuary.berkeley.edu.
