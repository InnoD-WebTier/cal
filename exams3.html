1. Attend the Actuary Exam P Prep DeCal

This exam prep course is led by our beloved decal course coordinator. They will be helping you with your exam studies with review lectures and problem discussions. If you need to take the class for units, you must obtain a CEC (Course Entry Code) from the decal course coordinator. Make sure you attend the class, because the course is quite intense and fast-paced.

2. Register for Actuary Exam P

Check for the application due date from forum announcements or upcoming events sections. SOA/CAS must RECEIVE (NOT POSTMARK) the application before the due date.

Most exams have online registration available and those that require paper applications can be found on the SOA/CAS websites. You can learn about exam registration through the SOA’s exam site or through the CAS’s exam site.

More information regarding deadlines, instructions, refund policies, etc. for the Exams P/1, FM/2, MFE/3F, C/4 is also available at the Be An Actuary’s website.

3. Attend the Mock Exam and Study Groups

This will help to prepare you for the actual exam. The test is very long and without the right preparation, most likely you’ll become mentally fatigued during the test and end up not doing as well as you like. From our past experience, those who attend a majority of the study groups tends to do better on the exam. For the times of the mock exam and exam study groups, check forum announcements or upcoming events sections.

4. Register for Actuary Exam P

Check for the application due date from forum announcements or upcoming events sections. SOA/CAS must RECEIVE (NOT POSTMARK) the application before the due date.

Most exams have online registration available and those that require paper applications can be found on the SOA/CAS websites. You can learn about exam registration through the SOA’s exam site or through the CAS’s exam site.

More information regarding deadlines, instructions, refund policies, etc. for the Exams P/1, FM/2, MFE/3F, C/4 is also available at the Be An Actuary’s website.

5. Let Us Know How You Did!

We are always trying to improve on our decal. We would really appreciate it if you could e-mail calapps@gmail.com with your score after receiving it. The scores would only be used as statistical data for future references. We would also really appreciate it if you would also give us your comment or thoughts about the decal in your e-mail.

Hopefully everyone will have successfully passed your exam I after following these steps. If not, don’t give up and try again, it’s always easier the second time around.
